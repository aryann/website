About

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Aryan. I'm a software engineer based in Seattle, WA. I currently work at Restaurant Brands International; previously, I worked at Google.

During my time at Google from 2012 to 2021, I contributed to various projects, including BigQuery, App Engine, Google Analytics, and Compute Engine.

I graduated from the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington in 2011.

If you'd like to reach me, you can email me at aryan.naraghi@gmail.com. Elsewhere on the web, you can find me on GitHub and LinkedIn.
